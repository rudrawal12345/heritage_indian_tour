Famous Places to Visit in Rajasthan

  • Jaipur

    The "Palace of Winds" is a five-story palace with 953 small windows and balconies.

  • Udaipur Fort

    A UNESCO World Heritage site, udaipur Fort is one of the largest forts in the world.

  • udaipur Lake

    Considered one of the most sacred lakes in India, udaipur Lake is surrounded by 52 ghats.

  • jodhpur

    The City Palace complex is a blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture, and houses a museum.
